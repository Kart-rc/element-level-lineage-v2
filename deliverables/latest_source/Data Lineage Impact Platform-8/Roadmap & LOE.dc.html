04The 15 use cases

Each use case is a full vertical slice — several services, data apps and platforms — delivered end to end: lineage capture, impact analysis, and PR gating. Use Case 01 is proven in Chapter 1; the remaining 14 onboard in waves whose velocity ramps — one lane (Team 3) while the platform is completed, two lanes once Team 2 joins at M13 — rather than a flat rate.

Why the velocity is credible
One 5-engineer lane has ~10 person-months per 2-month wave; a use case costs ~{{ perSlicePm }} pm — so ~2 concurrent per lane, rising to ~3 as tooling matures. One lane delivers 7 by M13; two lanes deliver the remaining 7 by M18.
New patterns will appear
New use cases will surface lineage patterns the collectors don't yet handle. Team 1 runs a rolling capability-build lane from M11: gaps found by onboarding become collector work without stalling the waves.
Onboarded ≠ done
Live use cases need support — drift, breakages, questions. From M14 Team 1 formally owns platform support & maintenance, so onboarding lanes never get pulled back into sustaining work.
